About BUET

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) is a public university in Dhaka, Bangladesh that offers over 66 graduate programs and is known as a leader in engineering education and research. BUET was founded in 1876 as the Dacca Survey School and became a university in 1962. It is the oldest institution in Bangladesh for the study of engineering, architecture, and urban planning. BUET has a top-ranked research faculty and regularly publishes work in international journals and conferences. BUET is a technological research and innovation hub that provides expert services to infrastructure development projects. Faculty members work with industry and the government to address problems and formulate national policies and strategies. BUET is also a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities and has collaborations with hundreds of universities and industries at home and abroad. BUET offers degrees in civil, mechanical, electrical, chemical, agricultural, and textile engineering, as well as diplomas in civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering. The QS World University Ranking 2024 ranked BUET fifth among Asian universities.
